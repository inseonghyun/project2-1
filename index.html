Silk Jacquard Fabric

2023.08.01
Silk thread is an extremely fine animal fiber. Where one strand of hair is 50 to 60 denier (a unit of thread thickness), in comparison silk is the thinnest of threads at 2 to 3 denier and when you twist numerous strands together you finally get a thickness that is over 10 denier. When you take this thin thread and create fabrics that are densely woven, it can create beautiful patterns and textures, however on the other hand it is true that it can be very difficult to handle.
